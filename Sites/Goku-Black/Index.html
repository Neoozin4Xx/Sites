GOKU BLACK

A Supreme Kai apprentice who grew disillusioned with mortals and their violent nature. After stealing the body of Son Goku, he became the ultimate warrior - Goku Black. Now he travels across timelines to cleanse the multiverse of all mortal life, believing only the gods are worthy of existence.

"Mortals are nothing but arrogant insects, squabbling over meaningless conflicts. Their very existence is an affront to the gods. I will purge this universe clean, and from its ashes, a new divine order will rise."
