Attack on Titan

In a world where humanity faces extinction at the hands of man-eating Titans, a young boy named Eren Yeager vows to reclaim what has been taken from him.

The Story

A Battle for Survival

For over a century, humanity has lived inside cities surrounded by enormous walls that protect them from the Titans, gigantic humanoid creatures who devour humans seemingly without reason.

The story centers around Eren Yeager, his adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their childhood friend Armin Arlert, whose lives are changed forever after a Colossal Titan breaches the wall of their home town.

Vowing revenge and to reclaim the world from the Titans, Eren and his friends join the Scout Regiment, an elite group of soldiers who fight Titans outside the walls.

The Titans

Pure Titans

Pure Titans are the most common type of Titan. They range from 3 to 15 meters in height and mindlessly consume humans. They are unable to harden their skin and have no special abilities.

Nine Titans

The Nine Titans are Titans that can be controlled by humans who have the "power of the Titans." Each has unique abilities and appearances. These include the Attack Titan, Founding Titan, Colossal Titan, Armored Titan, Female Titan, Beast Titan, Jaw Titan, Cart Titan, and War Hammer Titan.

Abnormal Titans

Abnormal Titans are Pure Titans that display unusual behavior or intelligence. They may ignore humans to pursue specific targets or exhibit patterns that make them more unpredictable and dangerous.
