Jujutsu Techniques & Cursed Energy

Explore the fascinating world of Jujutsu sorcery

Cursed Energy Fundamentals

Cursed Energy is the energy that flows through all living beings, generated from negative emotions such as fear, regret, hatred, and anger.

Understanding Cursed Energy

Only Jujutsu Sorcerers can control and manipulate cursed energy. Non-sorcerers generate cursed energy but cannot see or control it.

Sorcerers train to visualize and control their cursed energy flow, allowing them to enhance physical abilities, create barriers, and execute techniques. Advanced sorcerers can even use Reverse Cursed Technique to heal wounds by reversing the flow of cursed energy.

Jujutsu Techniques Categories

Innate Techniques

Unique abilities inherited within certain families or individuals.

Domain Expansion

The ultimate application of Jujutsu, creating a pocket realm where the user's technique is guaranteed to hit.

Binding Vows

Restrictions placed on oneself or others to gain increased power.

Reverse Cursed Technique

Advanced technique that reverses the flow of cursed energy, primarily used for healing.

Character Techniques

Gojo Satoru: Limitless & Six Eyes

The Limitless technique allows Gojo to manipulate space at the atomic level, creating "infinity" between himself and his target. Combined with his Six Eyes, he can use techniques like:

  • Infinity: A passive barrier that slows down attacks to never reach him
  • Blue: Creates a vacuum by pulling space together
  • Red: Repels space to push objects away with great force
  • Purple: Combines Blue and Red to create a destructive void
  • Domain Expansion: Unlimited Void - Floods targets with infinite information

Cursed Tools & Weapons

Cursed Tools are objects imbued with cursed energy, often used by sorcerers to enhance their abilities.
